About me

I am a visiting assistant professor of mathematics at University of California, Riverside .

Before this, I finished my PhD at the University of Chicago under the supervision of Victor Ginzburg.
My research interests are in representation theory, algebraic geometry and combinatorics.
I did my undergraduate studies at Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur.
