During high school, I loved participating in math olympiads and other competitions. I participated in the 54th International Mathematical Olympiad at Santa Marta, Columbia and won a bronze medal.
Before that, I qualified the Regional Mathematics Olympiad (RMO) in 2010 and the Indian National Mathematical Olympiad (INMO) in 2011 and 2012, and was selected for attending the IMO Training Camp (IMOTC) at HBCSE, Mumbai from 2011 to 2013.

Olympiad Training

I still quite enjoy solving problems from the IMO and Putnam whenever I take time off research and I am always happy to discuss problems that I have solved and provide advice to high-school students for training for olympiads.
