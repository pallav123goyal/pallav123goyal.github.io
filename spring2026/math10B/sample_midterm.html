Exam Questions

Question 1

Compute the double integral:

\[ \int_{0}^{1}\int_{y}^{1} e^{y/x} \, dx \, dy. \]

Question 2

Set up a triple integral to compute the volume enclosed between the surfaces \(x=0\), \(y=0\), \(x+2y=6\), \(z=0\), and \(x+y+z=0\). Evaluate this integral.

Question 3

Find the volume of the solid enclosed by the surfaces \(x^2 + y^2 = \frac{1}{5}z^2\) and \(z = 5 + \sqrt{5 - x^2 - y^2}\) above the \(xy\)-plane.

Question 4

Evaluate the integral:

\[ \iint_{D} xy \, dA \]

where \(D\) is the region in the first quadrant bounded by the curves:

  • \(x^2 + y^2 = 4\)
  • \(x^2 + y^2 = 9\)
  • \(x^2 - y^2 = 1\)
  • \(x^2 - y^2 = 4\).
